## Sunday Mornings at 9:30 AM

The Sunday morning adult Bible study is at 9:30 AM in the Sanctuary between services. The current study is Didache, led by Pastor Jeong.

## Wednesday Evenings at 7:00 PM

We have two new Wednesday evening Bible studies starting.  Pastor Jeong is teaching “Foundations of the Faith,” a broad overview of Lutheran teaching.  And Pastor Miller is teaching “Hitchhiking with Prophets” using a book of the same name by Chad Bird.  Both begin at 7:00 PM, with Pastor Jeong in the Sanctuary and Pastor Miller in the Priscilla room.

## First Thursdays at 7:00 PM

The Women’s Bible study is held on the first Thursday of each month at 7:00 PM in the Music Room. The current study is Isaiah 40-55, led by Karen Miller.

## Second Saturdays at 9:00 AM

Breakfast Bible studies are held on the second (or third) Saturday of most months and meets in the Fellowship Hall. Breakfast is offered at 8:30 AM followed by the study. There is usually an option to participate over Zoom as well. The presenter and topic are announced in the Sunday bulletin and in Redeemer News. Meeting information and a Zoom link are posted under Upcoming Events.
